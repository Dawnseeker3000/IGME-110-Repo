# My recomended video games!

all games will be linked at the bottom of this to fufill the "list of links" requirement

## Outer wilds
The story game, puzzle exploration game focused on uncovering the story as you play
- amazing story, has made many players cry
- every corner of the explorable world has things to find
  - nothing is empty

## Rimworld
Colony sim with near infinate potential.
- large modding community.
- Fairly optimized, the engine can handle a lot.

## Hardspace: Shipbreaker
Story based game about breaking down spaceships in zero g
- easy core elements with a lot of depth
- fleshed out world
- influencial story
- good graphics

## Hollow knight
influential Metroidvania
- easy core
- amazing art

## Rain world
Best way to describe this game is a exploration game using a ecosystem simulation
- very detailed creature interaction
- world feels alive
